Welcome to Ring Of Elysuim

Ring of Elysuim is a Free to play battle royal game. that puts players inside of a city which has snow mountains and trying to outrun a brutal storm called Ymir, The only way out is a helicopter which can only take four players out but the fight to the helicopter is the exciting part of the adventure because players get climbing gear, a snow board, or a hang glider to traverse the snowy terrain and adapt to intense situations! Which you can play up to four of your friends in a survive of the fittest and come out the only survivors out of the rest of the survivors!
